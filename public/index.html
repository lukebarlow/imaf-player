My New Mix

This is an idea for a simple way to embed multitrack audio in a web page in a way which is compact and visually distinctive. It takes a lead from the way soundcloud allows you to embed tracks on your own website. However, in this case, we can remix the audio how we like.

To remix the audio, simply click the 'remix' button to 'open up' the track, then click and drag vertically to adjust the volumes of the different tracks. Click on the 'remix' button a second time to 'close' the track. The relative volumes of the different tracks will be represented in the coloured stripes you can see.

The HTML to embed this on your own website is extremely simple. You first embed the javascript and stylesheet in the head section of your web page

            <link rel="stylesheet" type="text/css" href="css/mix.css">
            <script type="text/javascript" src="js/mix-min.js"></script>

And then just place the <mix> tag anywhere in your html. It has a single attribute, src. So, for example 

            <mix src="../audio/logan3.ima"></mix>
